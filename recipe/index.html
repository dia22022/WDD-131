Apple Crisp

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients together in a bowl.
  3. Beat the eggs and add them to the mixture.
  4. Put the batter in a baking dish and bake for 20 minutes.
