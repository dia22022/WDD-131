Overview

Purpose

My personal site will be about my photography. I will cover many different types of photography and talk about them and why they are important.

Audience

My intended audience is for those who love or wish to explore and learn more about photography.

Dynamic elements

I will use Javascript to change the background image to another image, use of buttons, and contact button.
